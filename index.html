Whole30 Quick Reference

Yes — Eat These

  • Meat & poultry
  • Seafood & fish
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables
  • Fruit (in moderation)
  • Natural fats (olive oil, ghee, coconut oil)
  • Nuts & seeds
  • Herbs, spices & seasonings
  • Potatoes & sweet potatoes
  • Coffee (black)

No — Avoid These

  • Added sugar (real or artificial)
  • Alcohol (any form)
  • Grains (wheat, rice, oats, corn)
  • Legumes (beans, peanuts, soy)
  • Dairy (milk, cheese, yogurt)
  • Carrageenan, MSG, sulfites
  • Baked goods (even with compliant ingredients)
  • Pasta, bread, tortillas
  • Processed snack foods

Gray Area — Use Sparingly

  • Fruit juice (as ingredient only)
  • Dried fruit
  • Compliant chips & fries
  • Compliant pancakes or treats
  • Nut butters (check labels)
  • Larabars & RXBARs
  • Coconut aminos
  • Store-bought dressings (read labels)
  • Smoothies (eat your food)
